Pay your taxes securely at irs.gov/directpay - a free & secured website of Internal Revenue Service (IRS). Individuals can pay their Federal taxes easily or can schedule it in advance for 30 days.


How can I pay my taxes directly to IRS online?

  • Go to irs.gov/directpay on your web browser.
  • Select any Payment method on IRS Direct Pay page.
  • Put in required details - Social Security Number (SSN) or Individual Taxpayer Identification Number (ITIN), date of birth.
  • Make your Payment by entering Bank account details and mode of payment.
  • Now, submit your Payment details. And choose to pay immediately or you can schedule it for up to 30 days in advance.
  • Confirm the details you've entered and click 'Submit' button to allow IRS for processing Payment.
  • You'll get a confirmation mail upon successfully submitting your details.
